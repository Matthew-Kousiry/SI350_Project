About Bruce

Bruce the Golden Retriever has been fondly remembered as a true "man's best friend." Aside from his carefree nature and loving attitude, Bruce is a decorated combat veteran seeking a forever home from his fans in the world of cyberspace. By combat I mean mericlessly giving you love and affection without your consent. In a word, Bruce is a true example of unconditional love in the modern world.
