Pet the Dog!

What's This?

Bruce is an amazing, curious, lovable golden retriever in need of lots of love! Help show that to him by taking part in the challenge of being his biggest friend!

How to Play

Login to keep track of your love for Bruce. Each time you virtually pet him, you review one point. The player with the most points earns the opportunity to meet Bruce in person!

Why Bruce?

Because hes a dog, hes cute, and a literal peach. And because this was the only kind of dog that wouldnt completely overtake the screen or be too small to see.
